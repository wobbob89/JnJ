About

We cover the build, the fit-out and the finish under one name.

The work shown here runs from structure and renovation through to bathrooms, kitchens, plastering, tiling, carpentry and the final details that make the job look right.

Approach

Less handover, less confusion, better control of the end result.

The jobs that suit us are the ones that need more than one trade and need the work to stay joined up. We are not interested in a messy handover where the finish gets treated like somebody else's problem.

If the job needs structure, bathrooms, kitchens, plastering, tiling, carpentry and the final detail work, we would rather keep that standard in one place.

Build

Extensions, renovations and structural work

We handle the heavy stage of the job properly instead of treating it like a separate project.

Fit-Out

Bathrooms, kitchens, storage and room-by-room work

The interior stage matters just as much as the shell, so it is part of the same standard.

Finish

Plastering, tiling, carpentry, roofing and final detail

The last part of the job is not an afterthought. It is what people see every day.
